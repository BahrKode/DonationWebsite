Personnel

Rufino Sendejo
President & General Manager
Pastor Sendejo is President and General Manager of Radio Libertad. He has been in the Ministry for 31 years with 20 of those dedicated to his Radio Ministry. He is married and has three children and two grandchildren.
   
   
   
Rosie Sendejo
Assistant General Manager
Sister Rosie Sendejo is the Assistant General Manager and wife of Pastor Sendejo. She has been in the Ministry for over 31 years. She has an afternoon Prayer Program on Radio Libertad from 3:30-6:00 pm.
